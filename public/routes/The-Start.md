# riding4GBS-The-Start

Riding for GBS challenges cyclists with a 384.5 km journey through dramatic mountain passes and valleys, climbing nearly 7,300 meters at gradients up to 20.7%. This route tests endurance while offering breathtaking vistas, from lowland forests to alpine peaks, uniting riders in the fight against Guillain-Barré Syndrome.

## Segments

### 1. Ascent to the Sky

This segment is a relentless climb toward Pal Pass, where steep gradients and narrow roads demand focus. Riders will push through pine-scented valleys, rewarded with panoramic views of the Pyrenees as they approach the summit's 2,102-meter mark.

- **Direction**: W
- **Distance**: 154.8 km
- **Elevation Gain**: 3119 m
- **Max Gradient**: 20.7%
- **Terrain**: Rugged mountain trails winding through dense forests and open alpine slopes.

### 2. Descent into the Unknown

A thrilling descent follows a grueling ascent, with sharp turns and breathtaking drops revealing hidden mountain villages. The route weaves through ancient stone bridges and wildflower meadows, blending challenge with serenity.

- **Direction**: W
- **Distance**: 123.1 km
- **Elevation Gain**: 2659 m
- **Max Gradient**: 17.6%
- **Terrain**: Technical switchbacks and steep descents through rocky terrain.

### 3. Echoes of the Peaks

This leg climbs toward Bonaigua Pass, where the air thins and the landscape becomes otherworldly. Cyclists will navigate winding paths that seem to touch the sky, with every pedal stroke echoing the resilience of those fighting GBS.

- **Direction**: NW
- **Distance**: 68.9 km
- **Elevation Gain**: 1513 m
- **Max Gradient**: 15.5%
- **Terrain**: Majestic high-altitude roads flanked by towering pines and sheer cliffs.

### 4. Final Descent

The final stretch is a thrilling descent, with momentum building as riders carve through sun-dappled forests. The route ends at 719 meters, marking the triumph of perseverance and the start of a new journey for GBS.

- **Direction**: NW
- **Distance**: 37.8 km
- **Elevation Gain**: 8 m
- **Max Gradient**: 13.4%
- **Terrain**: Smooth, fast descents through rolling hills and quiet valleys.
